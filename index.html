من نحن؟

We are one of the leading producer of base oil SN 150,SN 350 and SN 500 in iraq. Our base oil categorized in group I near to group II. It is used to manufacture products including lubricating grease , motor oil , metal processing floats and many other applications . In our factory we produce diverse grades of base oil using vacuum distillation and solvent extraction technology with modern production facilites.
